objectives of a website

  • Regardless of your business start by listing specific website goals and corresponding abjectives that fit your overall marketing strategy and capabilities. Example might include:

objective 1

  1. search engine optimisation
  2. Well-organised content
  3. User- friendly
  4. Effective calls to action
  5. Increase conversion rate

goal:Increase sales

objective 2

  1. Providing quality content on your website
  2. Regularly adding new information
  3. Establish trust
  4. Marketing your site on other websites and social media

Goal:Becoming an authoritative resource

Objective 3

  1. E-mainly marketing lists
  2. Online support(live chart)
  3. And content designed to give your visitor reason to come back

goal:improve interaction with existing and potential customers
